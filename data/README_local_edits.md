This app stores **Portugal schedule edits** (drag/drop order, added items, checklist completion)
in your browser using `localStorage` so you can experiment safely without overwriting
`data/itinerary.json`.

If you ever want to reset edits, clear site data for `http://127.0.0.1:5000`
in your browser settings, or remove the `localStorage` keys:

- `portugal_itinerary_v1`
- `portugal_trip_mode_v1`
- `portugal_checklist_v1`
